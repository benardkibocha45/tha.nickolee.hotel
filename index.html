Welcome to Tha Nickolee Hotel

Located in the heart of Nanyuki, Tha Nickolee Hotel blends modern comfort with local charm. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, our luxurious rooms, full breakfast, and serene atmosphere ensure a memorable stay. Enjoy access to an indoor pool, two restaurants, a coffee lounge, and proximity to Mount Kenya, Ol Pejeta Conservancy, and other major attractions.

Our Services

Explore Our Amenities

Comfortable Rooms

Enjoy 68 modern rooms including Standard and Deluxe options, all with private bathrooms, blackout curtains, Wi-Fi, and daily housekeeping.
Dining Experience

Choose from two on-site restaurants and unwind at our two bars or coffee shop. Enjoy complimentary breakfast served daily from 6:00 AM to 10:00 AM.
Indoor Pool

Relax and rejuvenate in our clean, temperature-controlled indoor swimming pool — perfect for both leisure and fitness.
Fitness & Wellness

Stay active with our fitness facilities or unwind with spa and wellness treatments available on request.
Event Hosting

Host meetings, events, or private parties in our modern event spaces with catering and full staff support available.
Free Secure Parking

Enjoy the convenience of complimentary secured self-parking with accessible spaces and elevator access.
